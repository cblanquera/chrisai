# Greenfield Grill Review Model

## Report Structure

```markdown
# Final Implementation Readiness Review

## Reviewed Artifacts

## Executive Readiness Assessment

Ready | Ready With Conditions | Not Ready

## Blockers

## High Findings

## Medium Findings

## Low Findings

## Open Questions

## ADR Challenges

## Cross-Document Inconsistencies

## Security And Permissions Risks

## Scaling And Operational Risks

## Lock-In And Migration Risks

## Required Updates Before Freeze
```

## Finding Format

```markdown
## FINDING-000: Title

Severity: BLOCKER | HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW | QUESTION

### Evidence

### Risk

### Strongest Argument Against Current Plan

### Required Resolution

### Affected Artifacts
```

## Review Rules

- Ground each finding in the reviewed artifacts.
- Do not invent missing requirements.
- Distinguish unsupported assumptions from explicit decisions.
- Treat unresolved security, permission, persistence, and operational ambiguity
  as high risk until proven otherwise.
- Prefer fewer strong findings over a long list of speculative objections.
